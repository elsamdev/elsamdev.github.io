Game Rules:

  • Each player starts with a deck of 12 cards and a hand of 3 cards.
  • Select one card from your hand and one from your opponent's hand to battle.
  • Cards have different types with unique abilities:
    • Normal: Basic attack and health
    • Healer: Heals itself each turn
    • Defender: Has a shield that regenerates
    • Poison: Applies poison damage over time
    • DoubleAtk: Attacks an additional random enemy card
  • Battle continues until one player has no cards left in hand and deck.
